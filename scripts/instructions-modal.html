Instructions:

Moving
When starting in a room you may do one of the following
·        Move into a hallways if no other player is occupying it
·        Take a secret passage to a diagonally opposite room (if there is one) and make a suggestion
·        If you were moved to the room by a suggestion made by another player, you may make a suggestion or perform one of the above 2 moves.
When starting in a hallway
·        Move to one of the 2 rooms accessible from the hallway and make a suggestion
If you cannot physically move your player from the current room they are in, due to blocked hallways, and you were not moved to your current room based on a suggestion, you simply lose your turn.
Making a Suggestion
When making a suggestion, you must be in the room that will be in your suggestion. You then move the player that you are suggesting to that room with you. You must also suggest a weapon. 
Falsifying a Suggestion
After a player has made a suggestion, the other players have the goal of disproving the suggestion. This starts with the player whose turn it is next. If they have a card in their hand that can disprove the suggestion, they share it with the person who made the suggestion. If that person can’t disprove the suggestion, then the next person tries to disprove it. This continues until one person can disprove the suggestion or everyone has had the chance to. If a player can disprove the suggestion with more than one card, they only share one card. It is important to remember that the player you control could also be the criminal.
Making an Accusation
Players are only allowed one accusation per game. An accusation can be made at any time during a players turn. Accusations are different than suggestions in that they don’t move players and other players do not disprove the accusation. After the accusation is made, the player who made the accusation will see the solution set. If they are correct than the game is over, if they are wrong then they no longer make any moves. There only roll going forward in the game will be to disprove suggestions using the cards in their hand.
